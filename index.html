My Career So Far

I studied Chemistry at the University of New South Wales and graduated in 2014. After graduation, I worked for nearly 7 years at a contract cosmetics manufacturer as a Quality Assurance Chemist and was later promoted to Senior QA Chemist. In late 2021 I felt that it was time for a career change and when I came across Academy Xi I decided to take the plunge and enroll.
